    Low carb makes a difference

    Choosing low carb food is the only way to guarantee a truly low sugar lifestyle.

    starch joined together =carbohydrates

    Carbs are found in many foods including traditional
    breads, potatoes, pasta, rice, cereal, honey, fruit and
    even milk!

    Carbohydrates consist of 2 components - Starch and
    Sugar (glucose). When carbohydrate foods are eaten
    and digested, the body breaks these down into glucose.
    (Glucose is the simplest form of sugar). So, even if you
    eat a product that claims to be "Sugar Free" or "Low
    Sugar" but it has carbs in, the carbs will break down in
    your body to become sugar anyway.
